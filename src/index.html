About the project

Australian businesses have begun to reopen their doors to the public. As part of this process, bars, cafes, restaurants and other businesses are required to collect the contact details of their patrons for the purposes of coronavirus contact tracing. CovidVault offers an open-source platform to make contact registration an effortless addition to COVIDSafe operations.

Prominent Branding

Your brand and logo are on prominent display to ensure your customers know they are supplying their details to the correct business

Minimal Data Collection

The app only collects the data deemed necessary by the government to enable contact tracing

Conditions of Entry

You can set conditions of entry that visitors must agree to for those times where there are concerns regarding hotspots or quarantine measures

Time Limits

Data is only stored for the minimum mandated time period as determined by the relevant state government

Regular Updates

The app is updated regularly with new features constantly being added with the assitance of suggestions by current account holders

Captive Adspace

Upon visitor check-in, there is an opportunity to provide customers with important COVID-19 information, promotions or just a message of gratitude for supporting your business during this difficult time

Why CovidVault?

Works out of the box

Once you have created your account, you can immediately start collecting data using CovidVault.

Data cannot be lost

Unlike hand-written, paper-based registers, a digital register cannot be accidentally damaged, discarded or destroyed. Once added, the contact details will remain in the CovidVault database until the storage period expires.

Customer details are safe

A publicly displayed register permits any visitor access to other customers' personal information. A back-of-house register could be compromised by an employee acting improperly. CovidVault ensures the register can only be accessed by your company's authorised contact.

No spam

Once stored, no one has access to customer details unless a formal request is made. As such, customers can rest assured that they will not be inundated by spam marketing phone calls or emails when they register their details on CovidVault.
